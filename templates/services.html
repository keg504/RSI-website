    Our Services

    RSI provides training and consultancy services

    Surgical Camps

    RSI offers surgical camps in rural and remote areas to help hospitals by invitation to conduct surgical camps for gasless laparoscopic and endoscopic urology services.

    Diagnostic Camps

    RSI offers diagnostic camps in rural and remote areas through nearby hospitals that can offer surgical care to diagnose surgical conditions in rural and remote areas.

    Online and Onsite Training Programs

    RSI offers Online and Onsite training programs in Rural Urology Practice and Gas Insufflation Less Laparoscopic Surgeries (GILLS) in association with the International Federation of Rural Surgeons (IFRS), the Association of Rural Surgeons of India (ARSI) and Martin Luther University.

    Medical Devices Consultancy Services

    RSI offers help with a human resources and equipment sharing program where rural surgeons contribute and get equipment that they share along with the human resources that are necessary for using the equipment

    Consultancy Services for Medical Devices Maintenance

    RSI offers consultancy services for refurbishing surgical equipment and maintenance of the surgical equipment.
