Services We Provide

The Surgical Camps

RSI offers surgical camps in rural and remote areas to help the rural hospitals based on invitations for conducting such surgical camps for innovative Gasless laparoscopic and endoscopic Urology services.

Diagnostic Camps

RSI offers diagnostic camps in rural and remote areas through nearby hospitals that can offer surgical care to diagnose surgical conditions in rural and remote areas.

Online and Onsite Training Programs

RSI offers Online and Onsite training programs in Rural Urology Practice and Gas Insufflation Less Laparoscopic Surgeries [GILLS] along with International Federation of Rural Surgeons [IFRS] and Association of Rural Surgeons of India [ARSI] and Martin Luther University.

Consultancy Services for Medical Devices

RSI offers consultancy services for refurbishing surgical equipment and maintenance of the surgical equipment.

Consultancy Services for Medical Devices

RSI offers help with Human resource and equipment sharing program where rural surgeons contribute and get equipment that they share along with the human resource that is necessary for using the equipment
