    Privacy Policy for Rural Surgery Innovations Private Limited

    Last Updated: 2025-03-08

    Introduction

    Rural Surgery Innovations Private Limited ("RSI", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services, website, or mobile applications.

    Information We Collect

    We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, which may include:

    • Personal identifiers such as your name, age, gender, and contact information
    • Medical information necessary for research and studies
    • Communication preferences and history
    • Other information you choose to provide us

    How We Use Your Information

    The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

    • To conduct medical research and studies
    • To improve our services and develop new medical innovations
    • To communicate with you regarding our services
    • To comply with legal obligations

    Data Security

    We implement strict security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Your data is stored in a secured environment to ensure confidentiality and integrity.

    Anonymity & Confidentiality

    All data collected is anonymized and stripped of any personal identifiers to ensure user privacy. We do not track or link any data to individual users.

    No Third-Party Involvement

    We do not involve any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks in data collection, storage, or processing. Your personal information is never sold, rented, or leased to third parties. Your information is used exclusively within RSI for the purposes stated in this policy.

    Your Rights

    Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

    • Access the personal information we hold about you
    • Request deletion of your information
    • Opt-out of providing data at any time.

    Changes to This Privacy Policy

    We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

    Contact Us

    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
