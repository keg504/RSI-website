    Laptop Cystoscope

    RSI produces the innovative Laptop Cystoscope that does away with the need for the telescope along with monitor, light source and camera that are necessary for conventional cystoscopies. Diagnostic cystoscopies are possible with the Laptop Cystoscope which could also be used for minor procedures.
